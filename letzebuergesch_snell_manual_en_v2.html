What mastering all 3 levels gives you

The Sproochentest requires A2 oral expression and B1 listening comprehension. This course is structured to cover both — completely and systematically.

Students who fully master all three levels will have active command of all vocabulary and conversational situations that appear in the official Sproochentest, as administered by the Institut national des langues Luxembourg (INLL).

The course covers 100% of the official exam topics: self-introduction, family, daily routine, work, housing, health, transport, Luxembourg society and civic life.

✅ Full mastery of all 77 lessons provides the solid language foundation that is both necessary and sufficient to pass the Sproochentest and qualify for Luxembourg citizenship.

What the Sproochentest actually tests

Sproochentest — Official Format (INLL)

Institut national des langues Luxembourg

Part 1 — Speaking
A2 ← Block 2

Oral interview with an examiner. You choose one of two topics, then describe a photo. Topics: self-introduction, family, daily routine, work, hobbies, housing, health, transport.

⚠️ The exam is 100% oral — no reading, no writing. Block 2 of this course is built entirely around these speaking situations.

Part 2 — Listening
B1 ← Block 3

Three audio recordings (~25 min): a news item, a conversation, a presentation. Multiple-choice comprehension questions.

✅ Block 3 of this course trains precisely these three listening formats — with the vocabulary and topic range of the official exam.

Record yourself (up to 10s), then compare with ▶ Léon and ▶ Me. The single most important feature for passing the speaking exam.

💡 The Sproochentest speaking exam is a live conversation. Use 🎤 on every lesson — this is your daily oral exam simulation.
